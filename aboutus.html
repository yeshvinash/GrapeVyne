MISSION

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our mission is simple. To provide a decentralised platform to bring all of Australia's wines to the hands of all Australians (and eventually the rest of the world) the correct way. From wine producer to the consumer without the use of a middleman or wine reseller.

FUTURE

future Plans of Grapevyne

With the current structure of the business and goals for the future, we are tirelessly aiming to enable the following to happen

Provide all types of Alcohol

With emphasis on decentralisation, we hope to reach out to all distilleries, breweries and wineries within Australia to allow them to also use this technology.

Website Marketplace Expansion

We aim to provide this experience and technology through a website, to allow the ease of ordering from your computer without the need of an app. However security is our main focus for now.

Events Management

With the mission to unite Australia's wine industry and therefore alcohol industry, we aim to play a role in events planning and management around Australia.

Global Expansion

With our eyes set on the Australian alcohol industry, we have plans on taking this technology globally to enable this technology to play a part in decentralising the global alcohol market to benefit all.

Connecting Australia's Vineyards one "Vyne" At A Time
