ABOUT US

what is the Grapevyne wine?

Grapevyne consists of an ambitious team that hopes to cater for Australia's ever expanding wine industry through its cutting edge online technology. We aim to revolutionise the process of obtaining wine and directly connecting the consumer to the producer. As a passionate business, our team wants you to truly capture the essence of celebrations and reunions with your favourite wines being the present that YOU get to choose. With Grapevyne, both wineries and consumers will evidently benefit using a simplified buying and delivery process that isn't limited to 'The Best', or a wine resellers warehouse storage.

FEATURES

Powerful features, Unlimited options

Browse and enjoy an ever expanding wine list that was curated using all the best Australia has to offer.

Personalised Wine List

A wine list that can be added to and altered at any moment to tailor to your needs, depending on what your palette requires.

Diverse alcohol selection

Our goal is to list wines produced by all wineries in Australia, expanding our lists and leaving the choice to you.

Decentralised

Without the use of warehouses on our end allows for a decentralised ecosystem bringing the power back into the hands of the wine makers.

Australian Made

Made by Australians for Australians, with the goal of creating a better self sustained ecosystem for the alcohol industry that isn't affected by foreign entities.

No Middle Man

Allowing the customer to directly connect with Australian wineries without the need of a middleman, better and honest prices that benefit both yourself and the winery.

Book Wine Tastings

Book in-person wine tastings at your favourite wineries in the palm of your hand.

Advanced API & Database

Latest in online technology to seamlessly connect wineries to you, and you to them.

Global expansion

With our mission in mind we hope to bring this decentralised system internationally and grant all Australian winemakers the opportunity to reach international customers with ease.

Secure payment options

Specifically chosen for the safety of the consumer, all payment options come with AES-256 encryption to keep transactions completely secure.... All payment credentials that are saved are done so locally on your device, only to maintain the highest level of customer security and satisfaction.

Wine tours and events

We aim to play a role in wine tours and festivals when the times allow in the near future where we can create a united wine culture under Grapevyne.
